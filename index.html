The Teeter

THE SAFETY SEE-SAW

The Teeter’s inspiration came from watching children being helplessly hoisted into the air. To solve this problem, the dangerous “totter” has been removed. The Teeter sits safely on the ground where children can safely isolate with their electronic media, or sit quietly.

  • No unpredictable playmates throwing your children into the air or jumping of at the bottom
  • No screaming or raucous laughter
  • Feet stay safely on the ground where they belong
  • No dizziness or motion sickness
  • Sitting still has been beta tested by old people in lounge chairs
  • Boredom guaranteed!
